Peri Brimley

About

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the University of Wyoming in Laramie, Wyoming.

My research interests include various topics in environmental economics, specifically as it pertains to the development of environmental policy. My dissertation, Human, Environmental, and Economic Interactions: Quantitative Studies and Empirical Support, includes studies investigating the value of wildlife recreation in Grand Teton National Park, the impact of mis- and disinformation of political support for climate change policy, and the alignment of current wildfire policy with public risk preferences.

My CV is here. I can be reached at pbrimley@uwyo.edu.
